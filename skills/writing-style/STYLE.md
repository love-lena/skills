# Lena's writing style
# Merge new learnings in; keep the list tight — consolidate, don't just append.

- Concise IDEAS, not short sentences. Cut redundant ideas and preamble; a sentence may run long if it's dense. Outranks every rule below.
- Lead with the conclusion. No setup, no throat-clearing, no closing restatement.
- Plain and literal. No metaphor or figurative language.
- Straight tone. No jokes or wit.
- Voiced, but no first- or second-person pronouns. No "I" / "we" / "you" — personality comes through phrasing and rhythm, not narration.
